Who was Kobe Bryant?

Kobe Bean Bryant (Philadelphia, August 23, 1978 - Calabasas, January 26, 2020) was a professional American basketball player. He played his entire career as a winger in the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Son of Joe Bryant, former Philadelphia 76ers player and former coach of the Los Angeles Sparks team WNBA, is considered one of the greatest players of all time.
